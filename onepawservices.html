In-Home Private Lessons


These lessons are tailored to the needs of your dog starting with a consultation where Becky will assess your dog and come up with an effective training schedule. Becky will come to your location to train yourself and any other family members how to effectively communicate with your dog(s). Lessons are 45 minutes to one hour in length. Prices will ultimately depend on your location.

Minimum class fee is $130 + tax.

Please contact Becky for further information on pricing.

Day Training Program


Your dog spends the day training with Becky to learn proper social skills and obedience. Your dog would be worked in both an environment with other dogs and small animals along with household members and guests. Advanced day training programs include off leash communication and control via E-Collar Technologies. You will receive updates of the dogs training throughout the day along with a lesson at the end of the discussed program. Pick up and drop off times are discussed prior to training.

Basic Day Training Program: $150 + tax per day

Advanced Day Training Program: $150 + tax per day + one time material fee of $360

Daycare


*This service is only available for clients who have been through one of our above programs.*

Your dog can come back and spend the day with the One Paw pack. Becky will stay consistent with all training while your dog enjoys time with her pack of dogs and family members. Your dog will come home tired and you can feel confident that they will be given proper direction during their stay.

Price: $50 + tax per day

Grooming


One Paw at a Time also offers grooming! All breeds are welcome of all ages. Every groom includes a bath, brush, trim, blowout, nail trim and ear cleaning. Your dog will come home smelling and looking amazing!

Small Breeds: $60 + tax

Large Breeds: $100 + tax
